Frequently Asked Questions - Hair and Nail Salon

GENERAL QUESTIONS

Q: What services do you offer?
A: We offer a full range of hair services including haircuts, coloring, highlights, styling, perms, and treatments. We also provide comprehensive nail services including manicures, pedicures, gel services, and nail art. See our full service menu for detailed pricing.

Q: How do I book an appointment?
A: You can book an appointment by calling us, visiting the salon in person, or using our online booking system. We recommend booking at least one week in advance, especially for weekends and special occasions.

Q: Do you accept walk-ins?
A: Yes, we accept walk-ins based on availability. However, we strongly recommend making an appointment to ensure you get the time slot you prefer. Walk-ins may have to wait or may need to be scheduled for a later time.

Q: What are your operating hours?
A: We're open Monday through Saturday from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

PRICING QUESTIONS

Q: How much does a haircut cost?
A: Haircuts range from $35-$75 depending on the stylist's experience level, hair length, and complexity of the cut. Men's cuts typically start at $35, women's cuts at $45, and children's cuts at $25.

Q: What does hair coloring cost?
A: Full color starts at $80 and can go up to $150 depending on hair length and color complexity. Highlights range from $90-$160, and balayage typically costs $120-$200. Your stylist will provide a detailed quote during your consultation.

Q: How much are manicures and pedicures?
A: Classic manicures are $25-$35, gel manicures are $35-$50. Classic pedicures are $35-$50, and gel pedicures are $50-$70. Additional services like nail art, French style, or paraffin treatments have additional fees.

Q: Do you offer package deals?
A: Yes! We offer package deals for multiple services and group bookings. For example, cut and color packages, bridal party packages, and monthly membership options. Ask about packages when booking.

APPOINTMENT QUESTIONS

Q: How long does a haircut take?
A: A standard haircut typically takes 30-45 minutes. If you're also getting styling, allow 45-60 minutes. Children's cuts usually take 20-30 minutes.

Q: How long does hair coloring take?
A: Hair coloring appointments typically take 2-3 hours depending on the service. Full color usually takes 2 hours, while highlights or balayage can take 3-4 hours.

Q: How long do manicures and pedicures take?
A: Classic manicures take about 30-45 minutes. Gel manicures take 45-60 minutes. Classic pedicures take 45-60 minutes, and gel pedicures take 60-75 minutes.

Q: What is your cancellation policy?
A: We require 24-hour notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance may be charged 50% of the service cost. Same-day cancellations or no-shows may be charged the full service amount.

Q: What if I'm running late?
A: Please call us if you're running late. If you arrive more than 15 minutes late, we may need to reschedule or modify your service to fit the remaining time. We'll do our best to accommodate you.

Q: Can I bring my child to my appointment?
A: For safety reasons, children should be supervised at all times. If your child needs a service, please book an appointment for them as well. We offer children's haircuts with special pricing.

SERVICE QUESTIONS

Q: Do you use organic or natural products?
A: We carry a variety of professional products including some organic and natural options. Ask your stylist or technician about product options that fit your preferences and needs.

Q: Can I bring a photo of the style I want?
A: Absolutely! Bringing photos of styles you like is very helpful. However, keep in mind that results depend on your hair type, length, and texture. Your stylist will discuss what's achievable.

Q: Do you offer hair extensions?
A: Yes, we offer various types of hair extensions. Prices range from $150-$400 depending on the method and amount of hair. Book a consultation to discuss options.

Q: What should I do before my color appointment?
A: Come with clean, dry hair. Avoid using heavy styling products. If you're unsure, ask when you book. Your stylist will provide specific instructions based on your service.

Q: How long does nail polish last?
A: Regular polish typically lasts 5-7 days with proper care. Gel polish can last 2-3 weeks. Both depend on your activities and nail care routine.

Q: Can I get both hair and nail services in one visit?
A: Yes! We can coordinate appointments so you can have both services. Book in advance and let us know you'd like both so we can schedule accordingly.

PAYMENT QUESTIONS

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept cash, all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment methods like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Q: Should I tip?
A: Gratuity is appreciated but not required. Typical gratuity ranges from 15-20% of the service cost, similar to restaurant tipping.

Q: Do you sell gift certificates?
A: Yes! Gift certificates are available for any dollar amount or specific services. They never expire and make great gifts. Purchase in person or by phone.

SPECIAL OCCASIONS

Q: Do you do bridal hair and makeup?
A: Yes! We specialize in bridal and special occasion styling. Book at least 2-3 weeks in advance. We offer package deals for bridal parties. Consultations are recommended.

Q: Do you offer group bookings?
A: Yes, we can accommodate groups for special events, parties, or bridal parties. Book well in advance and ask about group discounts.

Q: Can I schedule a trial run for a special event?
A: Absolutely! We highly recommend trial runs for special events like weddings or proms. This ensures you love your look before the big day.

PRODUCTS AND AFTERCARE

Q: Do you sell hair and nail products?
A: Yes, we carry professional hair care products, styling tools, and nail care products. Your stylist or technician can recommend products based on your needs.

Q: How should I care for my color-treated hair?
A: Use color-safe shampoos and conditioners, avoid excessive heat styling, and protect your hair from sun damage. Your stylist will provide specific recommendations.

Q: How do I maintain my gel nails?
A: Moisturize your cuticles daily, avoid picking or biting, and use gloves for household chores. Schedule regular appointments every 2-3 weeks for maintenance.
