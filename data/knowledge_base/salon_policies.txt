Salon Policies and Information

Welcome to our hair and nail salon! We're delighted to have you. This guide contains important information about our policies and services.

OPERATING HOURS
We are open Monday through Saturday from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We recommend booking appointments in advance to ensure availability, especially for weekends and special occasions. Walk-ins are welcome based on availability.

APPOINTMENT POLICY

Booking: Appointments can be made by phone, in person, or through our online booking system. We recommend booking at least one week in advance for popular time slots.

Cancellation: We require 24-hour notice for appointment cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance may be subject to a cancellation fee of 50% of the service cost. Same-day cancellations or no-shows may be charged the full service amount.

Late Arrivals: If you arrive more than 15 minutes late, we may need to reschedule your appointment or modify your service to fit the remaining time. We will do our best to accommodate you.

Multiple Services: For appointments with multiple services (e.g., haircut + color + manicure), please allow 2-4 hours. We recommend scheduling these on weekdays when the salon is less busy.

PAYMENT POLICY

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment methods. Gratuity is appreciated but not required. Typical gratuity ranges from 15-20% of the service cost. Gift certificates are available for purchase and make excellent gifts.

GIFT CERTIFICATES

Gift certificates are available for any dollar amount or specific services. They never expire and can be used for any service or product in the salon. Gift certificates can be purchased in person or by phone.

CHILDREN

Children are welcome! We offer children's haircuts and have special pricing. For safety reasons, children must be supervised at all times. If your child needs special accommodations, please let us know when booking.

PRODUCTS

We carry a selection of professional hair and nail care products. Our stylists can recommend products based on your hair type, styling needs, and nail care routine. Products can be purchased during your visit.

ALLERGIES AND SENSITIVITIES

Please inform your stylist or nail technician of any allergies, sensitivities, or medical conditions before your service begins. This includes skin conditions, chemical sensitivities, and pregnancy. We use professional-grade products, but we want to ensure your safety and comfort.

SATISFACTION

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with your service, please let us know immediately or within 48 hours. We will work with you to make it right, which may include a complimentary adjustment or redo at no charge.

SPECIAL OCCASIONS

We love helping you look your best for special events! For weddings, proms, parties, or other special occasions, please book well in advance (at least 2-3 weeks). We offer package deals for bridal parties and group bookings.

PARKING

Parking is available in front of the salon and in nearby public parking areas. Street parking is also available on a first-come, first-served basis.

ACCESSIBILITY

Our salon is wheelchair accessible. If you have any special needs or require accommodations, please let us know when booking so we can ensure your visit is comfortable.

HYGIENE AND SAFETY

We maintain the highest standards of cleanliness and sanitization. All tools are properly cleaned and sterilized between clients. We follow all health department regulations and best practices for salon hygiene.
